# Decision Management

## Objective

Keep strategic and architectural thinking visible and auditable.

## Core Outputs

- open questions
- assumptions register
- decision backlog
- rejected ideas

## Working Rules

- Distinguish unresolved questions from accepted decisions.
- Record assumptions with validation intent.
- Capture rejected directions to avoid circular debate.
- Keep this layer separate from ADRs and execution logs.

## Placeholders To Fill

- pending decisions
- assumptions
- validation plans
- rejected ideas
- revisit triggers

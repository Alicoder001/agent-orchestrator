# Research Synthesis

## Objective

Turn raw research into usable strategic guidance.

## Core Outputs

- competitive landscape
- market thesis
- AI-native organization research
- workflow automation research
- virtual workspace research

## Working Rules

- Prefer synthesis over note dumping.
- Tie findings to strategic implications.
- Distinguish evidence from inference.
- Keep research outputs decision-oriented.

## Placeholders To Fill

- research questions
- comparison dimensions
- implications
- risks
- follow-up research gaps
